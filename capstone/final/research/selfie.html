article research

The city of Davis was founded in 1869. The city is noted for its "convergence of geographical and infrastructural elements" (Shelton 2021). Davis's core values are

Aspiration; Community; Cooperation; Compassion; Leadership; Innovation; Engagement; Global Citizenship; Stewardship; Vision; Optimism.

The Centennial Seal was installed in 2021 by designer Susan Shelton for Davis's 100 year anniversary. The seal is meant to commemorate all the Davis values and the city's history.
